Clinical Outpatient System

A comprehensive outpatient management platform that tracks the complete patient journey from registration to discharge with integrated billing, inventory, and reporting.

Explore Patient Journey

System Features

Patient Registration & Visit Creation

Register new patients and create visits with consultation fees automatically added to patient bills.

Triage & Medical History

Nurses record medical history and vitals, updating visit status to "In Triage" before doctor consultation.

Doctor Consultation & Diagnosis

Doctors perform physical examinations, enter diagnoses, and prescribe medications, lab tests, and imaging.

Integrated Billing & Inventory

All services are automatically billed. Medications are deducted from inventory batches when dispensed.

Comprehensive Reporting

Generate financial reports, patient demographics, and inventory audits with printing capabilities.

Patient Journey Flow

1
Registration
Patient details are captured and visit is created. Consultation fee is added to bill if applicable.
2
Waiting Triage
Visit status updated to "Waiting Triage" as patient awaits nurse assessment.
3
Nurse Encounter
Nurse records medical history and updates status to "In Triage".
4
Vitals Recording
Nurse enters patient vitals (BP, temperature, pulse, etc.) and visit awaits doctor consultation.
5
Doctor Consultation
Doctor performs physical examination, updates status to "In Consultation".
6
Prescription & Tests
Doctor prescribes medications, orders lab tests and imaging. All items are billed automatically.
7
Diagnosis & Summary
Doctor enters diagnosis and generates case summary, marking visit as complete.
8
Billing & Payment
Patient views comprehensive bill with all charges and makes payment.

Key Features

Patient Registration

Comprehensive patient registration with demographic details and automatic visit creation with billing.

Clinical Workflow

Streamlined patient journey from triage to consultation with status tracking at each stage.

Prescription Management

Digital prescription with automatic billing and inventory deduction from specific batches.

Automatic billing for consultations, medications, lab tests, and imaging services.

Supplier registration, procurement workflow, and batch-based inventory tracking with audit logs.

Analytics & Reporting

Comprehensive financial reports, patient demographics, and system activity logs with printing.

Inventory & Procurement Flow

1

Supplier Registration

Register suppliers with contact details and product categories. Approved suppliers appear in the supplier list.

2

Procurement Creation

Create procurement requests in draft status. Once approved, they move to active procurement list.

3

Supply Fulfillment

When supplies are delivered and marked complete, items are added to inventory with batch numbers.

4

Inventory Management

Medications are tracked by batch numbers. When prescribed to patients, inventory is automatically deducted from the appropriate batch.

5

Inventory Audit

Comprehensive audit trail showing stock in, stock out, adjustments with details of who made changes, when, and why.
